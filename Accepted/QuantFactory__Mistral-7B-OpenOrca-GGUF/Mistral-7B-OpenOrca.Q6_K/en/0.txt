The Page of Wands, with its connection to the Water element, represents a time of exploration and discovery within the emotional realm. This card invites you to delve into the depths of your feelings, seeking hidden truths and understanding the impact of your emotions on your daily life. As you navigate the waters of your inner world, remember to honor your emotional sensitivity as a tool for connection and self-discovery. Embrace the flow of emotions and allow them to guide you on your journey of self-awareness and growth.
