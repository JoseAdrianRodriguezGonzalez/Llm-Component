The Fire card represents the drive and willpower of the individual. It is associated with the energy, passion, and determination that ignites action. The energy of Fire is both destructive and constructive, capable of burning away what is old and obsolete to make room for new creations. This card invites the individual to reflect on how their drive and willpower manifest in their actions and decisions. It suggests a need to properly channel inner strength and authenticity, aligning with a higher purpose. The Fire card challenges the individual to recognize and properly channel their inner strength, fostering personal growth and transformation.
