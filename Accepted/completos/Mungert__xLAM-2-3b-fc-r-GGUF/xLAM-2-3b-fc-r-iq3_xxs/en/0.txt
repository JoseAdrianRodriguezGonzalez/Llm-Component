The Page of Wands invites you to explore the depths of your emotions, to immerse yourself in your inner world. It calls for introspection, to identify and understand your dominant emotions, how they influence your daily life, and your decision-making processes. The card suggests evaluating the impact of these emotions on your interactions with others, fostering deeper connections and understanding. Embrace your emotional sensitivity as a tool for connection and self-discovery, allowing the flow of emotions to guide you on your journey.
