In the realm of the Self-Knowledge Star Spread, the Earth card invites you to reflect on the tangible aspects of your existence. It prompts you to consider how your spiritual aspirations align with your daily practices, particularly in relation to your relationship with the material world, health, and the environment. This card challenges you to cultivate a balanced and sustainable approach that reflects your deepest values, ensuring that your actions are grounded in the solid foundation of Earth, nurturing personal growth and fulfillment.
