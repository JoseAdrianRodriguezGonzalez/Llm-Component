Strength, a card of empowerment and resilience, symbolizes the overcoming of obstacles through sheer will and determination. It embodies the strength to endure trials and the courage to stand tall against adversity. This card encourages you to harness your inner fortitude, to draw upon the resources within yourself to overcome challenges. Like a mountain climber scaling the final ridge, Strength reminds you that victory is within reach if you persecede with unwavering resolve. This card invites you to embrace your power, to recognize your ability to overcome any obstacle that stands in your way. It is a call to action, urging you to channel your strength into tangible outcomes, to manifest your aspirations into reality. Remember, true strength is not measured by physical prowess but by the ability to endure and triumph over adversity. Embrace this card as a symbol of your indomitable spirit, a reminder that you have the power to overcome any challenge that comes your way.
