The Page of Wands, when encountered in the context of Water, reveals a mirror-like reflection of your emotional landscape. It invites you to explore the depths of your feelings, much like diving into the ocean of emotions. This card encourages you to embrace your vulnerabilities and to understand that each emotion is a unique message from your subconscious. It prompts you to listen to the whispers of your heart, to embrace the fluidity of your emotions, and to find the strength in your ability to express and connect with others through empathy. This reading suggests that the journey of self-discovery is a dance of emotions, where each wave of feeling is a step closer to understanding the essence of your soul.
