The Strength card, when interpreted in the context of Earth, reveals a powerful guardian of stability and resilience. This card embodies the unwavering determination to overcome challenges and harness resources for growth. It symbolizes the strength to endure hardship and the ability to find strength in the very essence of one's being. This reading suggests that true strength lies in the capacity to endure and overcome obstacles, much like the earth that remains steadfast through the harshest of seasons. It encourages the individual to embrace their physical and emotional resources, to cultivate resilience, and to find the strength within to weather any storm. This card invites a journey of self-discovery, where the individual discovers their own inner strength and the resources they possess to overcome any challenge.
