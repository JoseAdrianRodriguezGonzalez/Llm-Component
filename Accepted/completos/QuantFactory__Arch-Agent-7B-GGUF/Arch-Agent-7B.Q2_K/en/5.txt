The Queen of Swords, when interpreted in the context of Earth, reveals a mirror-like reflection of your material and emotional resources. This card symbolizes the realm of practicality and the material world, where your deepest fears and desires are often manifested. It invites you to explore the tangible aspects of your life, much like diving into the ocean of your own resources. This reading suggests that your material and emotional resources are like the water that flows beneath the waves, revealing the hidden treasures and secrets that lie within. It encourages you to connect with your body and the physical environment, to honor the intuitive wisdom that guides your actions, and to find the balance that reflects your deepest values and aspirations. This card invites a journey of self-discovery, where you discover the true essence of your material and emotional resources and the hidden treasures that lie within your subconscious.
