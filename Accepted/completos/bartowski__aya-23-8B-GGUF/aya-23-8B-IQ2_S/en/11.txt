The Temperance card, a card of balance and harmony, signifies the journey towards balance and the integration of opposites. It is a card of moderation and harmony, reminding us of the importance of balance in all things. Like a gentle wind, this card signifies the soft nudge that guides us towards harmony and integration. It is a reminder that balance is key, and that through moderation, we find our path towards harmony and unity. This card is a call to action, urging us to embrace the wind of change and use it to guide us towards a more harmonious and balanced path.
