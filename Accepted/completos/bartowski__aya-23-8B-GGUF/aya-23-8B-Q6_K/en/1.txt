The Strength card, an Earth card, symbolizes the power to harness and direct one's emotions and desires. It represents the ability to tap into the stability and strength that comes from our connection with the physical world and our senses. This card encourages you to ground your aspirations and dreams in the tangible reality of your life. By aligning your spiritual goals with the material plane, you can create a solid foundation for your journey. The Earth card invites you to reflect on your relationship with your body, your resources, and your environment. It is a reminder to nurture and care for yourself and your possessions, and to use your strength and stability to create a life that reflects your deepest values and aspirations.
