The Page of Wands, in the realm of water, represents a messenger of emotions and feelings. This card invites you to delve into the depths of your inner self, exploring the ebb and flow of your emotions. It is a reminder to honor your sensitivity and intuition, as they guide you in understanding your emotions and their impact on your decisions and interactions with others. The Page of Wands encourages you to embrace your emotional world, fostering connection and self-discovery.
