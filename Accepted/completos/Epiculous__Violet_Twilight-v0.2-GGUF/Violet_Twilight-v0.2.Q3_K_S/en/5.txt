The Queen of Swords stands tall amidst the earthly realm, her blade gleaming with the clarity of truth. She embodies the essence of justice, cutting through illusions and revealing what lies beneath the surface. Her presence invites introspection, urging us to confront our shadows and forge a path guided by integrity and authenticity.
