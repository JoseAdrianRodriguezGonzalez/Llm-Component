The Page of Wands, a card of new beginnings and fresh perspectives, emerges from the watery realm of emotions. In this context, it suggests a journey of self-discovery, where the seeker must dive deep into the depths of their own psyche. The flowing waters of intuition and feeling guide the way, revealing hidden truths and forgotten desires. This card invites the exploration of the subconscious, where the seeds of creativity and passion lie dormant, waiting to be nurtured. The Page of Wands encourages the seeker to embrace their emotional nature, for it is through the ebb and flow of feelings that we find the strength to grow and transform.
