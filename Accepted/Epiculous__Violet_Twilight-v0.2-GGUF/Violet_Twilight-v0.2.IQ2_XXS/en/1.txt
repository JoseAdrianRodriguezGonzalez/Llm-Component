Earth's materiality, 
A ground for being 
Solid, steady, enduring,
Resisting, persisting
In the world's resources. 
Balance, sustaining,
Financed, maintaining 
Alignment with values,
Tilling, cultivating 
In the Self-Knowledge.

In the Self-Knowledge Star Spread, the Earth card invites reflection on how we interact with the material world and how we manage our resources. It suggests evaluation of our relationship with money, health, and the environment, promoting a balanced and sustainable approach that reflects our deepest values.
