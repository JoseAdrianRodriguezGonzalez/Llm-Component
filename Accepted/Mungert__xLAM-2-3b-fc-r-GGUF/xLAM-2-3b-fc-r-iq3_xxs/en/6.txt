The Ten of Cups card, in the context of the Fire card, invites you to explore the depths of your innermost desires and the transformative power of your will. It suggests that by aligning your authentic desires with a higher purpose, you can ignite a transformative journey that burns away the old to create space for new, vibrant expressions of self. This card challenges you to reflect on the motivations behind your actions and decisions, urging you to consciously channel your inner strength for the sake of personal growth and transformation.
