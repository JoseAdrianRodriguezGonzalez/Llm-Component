The Four of Cups, an Earth card, invites you to reflect on the tangible aspects of your existence. It symbolizes the materialization of your deepest desires and the capacity to sustain life. This card challenges you to evaluate your relationship with the material world, including your health, finances, and the environment. It encourages a balanced and sustainable approach that aligns with your spiritual aspirations. By nurturing your connection with the Earth, you can find inner peace and fulfill your deepest values.
