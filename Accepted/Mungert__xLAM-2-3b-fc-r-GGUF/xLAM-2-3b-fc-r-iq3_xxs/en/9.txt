The Three of Wands, in the context of the Earth card, invites you to reflect on the tangible aspects of your spiritual journey. It suggests that your spiritual growth is not just about abstract ideas but also about the physical manifestation of your beliefs and emotions. The card encourages you to consider how your actions and decisions impact the material world, including your health, finances, and the environment. It prompts you to evaluate your relationship with the material world and to seek a balanced and sustainable approach that aligns with your deepest values.
