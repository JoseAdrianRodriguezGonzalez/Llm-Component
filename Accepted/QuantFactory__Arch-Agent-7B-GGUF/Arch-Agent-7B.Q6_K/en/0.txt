The Page of Wands, a card of transformation and new beginnings, symbolizes the flow of emotions as a river of change. It whispers of the depths where hidden truths lie, urging a journey into the subconscious. This card invites you to embrace your feelings, to see them as a map to your true self. Like the river, emotions can guide you to new insights, revealing the strength within to overcome challenges. Embrace the ebb and flow, for in the ebb lies the potential for the next great surge. This card encourages you to trust your feelings, to let them lead the way to deeper understanding and connection. Remember, every emotion is a step towards self-discovery, a bridge to the intuitive wisdom that lies within.
