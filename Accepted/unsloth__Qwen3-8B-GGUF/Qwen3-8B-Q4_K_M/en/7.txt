A garden of forgotten possibilities lies dormant, its seeds buried beneath the weight of what was left behind. The air is thick with the scent of decay and the promise of renewal, yet movement is hesitant, as if the ground itself waits for a hand to stir its slumber. Shadows stretch long over the horizon, whispering of choices unmade and paths that linger just beyond reach. There is a tension between the weight of the past and the fragile hope of what might grow from it, a delicate balance between holding on and letting go. The world remains, steady and unyielding, offering its nourishment to those who dare to listen to the quiet hum of its depths.
