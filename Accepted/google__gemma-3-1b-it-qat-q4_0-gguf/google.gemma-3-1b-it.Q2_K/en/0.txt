The swirling depths of this card whisper of a current, a powerful, unseen force pulling you forward. It’s a moment of profound, unbidden feeling, a quiet unveiling of what lies beneath the surface of your being. A gentle, insistent pull towards the unknown, demanding you to surrender to the ebb and flow of your emotional landscape.  

Look into the depths – do you see a familiar face, or a distant, shimmering one?  The water holds a secret, a reflection of a truth you’ve been hesitant to acknowledge.  It’s a call to trust the voice of your heart, even when it’s a tempestuous one.  Let the current guide you, allowing the subtle shifts of your emotions to shape your path, without judgment, simply observing.
