The Strength card, in the context of the Earth element, represents the tangible manifestation of our beliefs and desires. It symbolizes the physical world and the challenges we face in manifesting our dreams. The querent's strength lies in their ability to overcome these obstacles and maintain stability in the face of change. The card suggests that the querent must be grounded and practical in their approach to life, focusing on the here and now. It invites us to consider how we navigate the complexities of the material world and the role we play in shaping our reality. The Strength card reminds us to embody our aspirations in the physical realm, to take decisive action, and to cultivate a sense of purpose and direction. It challenges us to consider the relationship between our inner world and outer world, and to find the balance between our spiritual and physical expressions.
