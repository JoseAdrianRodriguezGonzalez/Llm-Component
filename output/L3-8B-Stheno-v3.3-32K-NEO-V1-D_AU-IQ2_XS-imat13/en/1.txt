The Strength card, in the context of the Earth element, represents the tangible manifestation of our beliefs and desires. It symbolizes the physical world and the challenges we face in manifesting our dreams. The querent's strength lies in their ability to overcome obstacles and maintain stability in the face of adversity. This card invites us to consider the relationship between our inner world and the external reality. It prompts us to examine how well we are aligning our spiritual aspirations with our daily practices and the impact we have on the environment. The Strength card asks us to look within and assess our relationship with the physical world, as it is the point at which our beliefs and actions meet. It challenges us to consider the balance between our inner and outer worlds, and to make adjustments to create a more harmonious and sustainable approach to life.
