The Page of Wands, a card of emotional awakening, invites you to embark on a journey of self-discovery. Like a gentle current, your emotions are flowing, and this card encourages you to immerse yourself in their depths. It is a reminder that your feelings are a powerful force, offering insight and a connection to your true self. The Water card reflects the hidden depths of your subconscious, encouraging introspection and a deeper understanding of your emotional landscape. This reading is a call to honor your emotional sensitivity and trust in your intuitive wisdom. It is a reminder that your feelings are a tool for self-discovery and that exploring them can lead to a deeper understanding of yourself and your place in the world.
