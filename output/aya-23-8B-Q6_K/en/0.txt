The Page of Wands, a water card, reflects the emotional journey of self-discovery. It is a call to explore the depths of your soul and uncover the hidden secrets that lie within. The card symbolizes the flow of emotions, a river of feelings that can either empower or overwhelm. It is a reminder that your emotions are a powerful force, a mirror to your true self. By embracing your emotions and understanding their impact, you can navigate the turbulent waters of life with greater clarity and empathy. This reading encourages introspection and a deep dive into your subconscious, where you will find the intuitive wisdom that guides your path.
