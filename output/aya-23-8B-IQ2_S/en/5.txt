The Queen of Swords, a card of wisdom and discernment, signifies a journey of self-discovery and the power of discernment. It is a card of insight and clarity, reminding us of the importance of our intuition and the wisdom that lies within. Like a sharp-edged sword, this card shines a light on the path of truth and insight, cutting through the fog of uncertainty and leading us towards a path of clarity and understanding. It is a reminder that our intuition is a powerful tool, and by trusting our wisdom, we can navigate life's challenges with discernment and insight. This card is a call to action, urging us to embrace our inner strength and use our wisdom to guide our choices and decisions.
