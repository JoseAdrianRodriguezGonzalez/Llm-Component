The Four of Cups, a card of stability and growth, signifies the foundation of our dreams and the journey towards fulfillment. It is a card of patience and growth, reminding us of the importance of stability and the foundation upon which our dreams are built. Like a strong foundation, this card signifies the quiet work of nurturing and cultivating, reminding us that true growth comes from within. It is a reminder to trust the process and have faith in the journey, for it is through patience and perseverance that we reap the rewards of our labor. This card is a call to action, urging us to embrace the quiet work of preparation and the steady hand that guides our dreams towards fulfillment.
