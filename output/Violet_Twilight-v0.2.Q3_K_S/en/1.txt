The Strength card invites you to embrace your inner power and resilience. Like the lion tamed by the gentle touch of the maiden, you too possess the ability to transmute your energy into compassionate action. Trust in your innate strength and let it guide you towards harmony with yourself and the world around you. Remember that true power lies not in domination but in the capacity to hold space for both your light and shadow aspects. As you walk the earthly plane, ground yourself in the present moment and allow your essence to infuse your actions with purpose and grace.
