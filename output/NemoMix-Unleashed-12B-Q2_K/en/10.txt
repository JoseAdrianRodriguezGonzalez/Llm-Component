Emotions flow like rivers, carving paths through the landscape of our psyche. They mirror the depths of our subconscious, revealing secrets we've long forgotten. To dive into these waters is to connect with our true selves, allowing intuition to guide us through the currents of feeling. The turbulence of emotion is not to be feared, but embraced as a vital source of information about our needs and desires. As we immerse ourselves in our emotional world, we discover the power of empathy to forge connections with others, understanding shared experiences through the lens of feeling. This card invites introspection, encouraging us to explore the impact of our emotions on daily life and decision-making. It whispers that sensitivity is not weakness, but a tool for connection and self-discovery, inviting us to honor the wisdom of our emotional landscape.
