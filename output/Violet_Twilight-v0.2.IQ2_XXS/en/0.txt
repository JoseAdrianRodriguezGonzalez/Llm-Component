The Water card, Page of Wands, symbolizes the flow of emotions and the depths of the subconscious. It reflects the hidden depths of the psyche, revealing forgotten truths and secrets. Water acts as a mirror, facilitating connection with others through empathy and enabling a deeper understanding of shared experiences. The seekers are encouraged to immerse themselves in their emotional world, exploring dominant emotions and their impact on daily life. The card invites introspection to identify emotions and evaluate how they influence decision-making and interactions with others. The Water card suggests honoring emotional sensitivity as a tool for connection and self-discovery.
