The Earth card symbolizes the material plane and our tangible existence. It invites us to reflect on how our beliefs and emotions manifest into actions and physical reality. This card challenges us to align our spiritual aspirations with our daily practices, considering our relationship with money, health, and the environment. The Earth element represents solidity and immobility, providing fertile ground for personal growth and fulfillment. In the Self-Knowledge Star Spread, this card offers an opportunity to evaluate our interactions with the material world and our resources, promoting a balanced and sustainable approach that reflects our deepest values.
