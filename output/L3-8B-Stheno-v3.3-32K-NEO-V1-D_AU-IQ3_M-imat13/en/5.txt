The Queen of Swords stands tall, her regal presence a testament to the power of the mind. In the realm of the Earth, she is the embodiment of clarity, focus, and determination. Her sword, a symbol of discernment, cuts through the chaos of the material world, revealing the truth that lies beneath. The Queen's steady gaze reflects the importance of mental stability and emotional control. She reminds us that true strength comes from within, and that by mastering our thoughts and emotions, we can navigate the challenges of life with grace and resilience. The Queen's presence in the Self-Knowledge Star Spread invites us to examine our inner world, to confront our fears and doubts, and to emerge with a clearer understanding of ourselves and our place in the grand tapestry of existence.
