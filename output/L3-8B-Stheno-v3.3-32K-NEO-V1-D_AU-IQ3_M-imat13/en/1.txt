The Strength card, in the context of the Earth element, represents the power of manifestation and the connection between the spiritual and physical realms. It symbolizes the process of bringing forth the intangible into the tangible, the transformation of energy and potential into concrete forms. The Strength card suggests that the querent possesses the ability to tap into their inner strength and resilience, to draw upon the resources of the earth itself to support their growth and endeavors. This card invites the querent to embrace their connection to the material world, to harness the power of their physical presence and the resources available to them. It encourages a balance between the spiritual and the physical, between the inner and outer aspects of their being. The Strength card reminds the querent that their strength lies not only in their inner resolve but also in their ability to manifest their intentions and desires in the world of form.
